SignWriting CheatSheet

  1. Perspective: right=signer's right side, left=signer's left side
  2. Hand Shapes
    • white: palm faces signer
    • black: palm faces away
    • black and white stripes: palm faces the side
    • disconnected fingers: fingers point forward instead of up (floor plane)
    • circle=open fist ('o' sign)
    • square=closed fist ('s' sign)
  3. Movement
    • Arrows
      • single line: forward/back plane (floor plane)
      • double line: up/down plane
      • white head=left hand, black head=right hand
      • thick part of arrow (tail or just below arrowhead) is closer to the body
    • Modifiers
      • *: hand makes contact
      • ~: ???
      • +: hold
      • #: strike
      • @: rub
      • dot-circle: brush
      • black dot over finger=joint closes
      • white dot over finger=joint opens
      • \/ = bend fingers (down=closed,up=open, double=drum fingers)
      • dot in arrow: toward the body diagonally
  4. Face: circle
  5. Torso: thick black bar
