About SkySigns

How to Use

  1. Go to the Load Data page.
  2. With an Internet connection, click the Load Signs and wait patiently
  3. Do the same with Load US Dictionary
  4. Go back to the Word Search page and type in a word, and if found, click to see the sign.

SignWriting

SignWriting was developed by Valerie Sutton to express dance positions and movement. Since then, it has been adapted for transcribing sign languages around the world.

It may look confusing at first, but it's very easy to learn. The SignWriting website has a lot of documentation, but the best primer (until I can incorporate one into this app) is in PDF available on the site at http://www.signwriting.org/spain/lecciones/.
